LETTER FROM THE PRESIDENT

Congratulations on taking the next steps toward pursuing your professional goals with Alpha Kappa Psi.

Here at the Alpha Zeta chapter of the University of Southern California, our brothers’ success is our mission. We strive to develop our brothers into principled business leaders through carefully-tailored professional lessons that transcend beyond their undergraduate years. These lessons in addition to a strong network of lifelong brothers equip our members with an arsenal of resources and experiences in which they can leverage to tackle any professional endeavor that lies ahead. It is no surprise that our brothers are consistently successful in placement at the best firms out there.

While professional excellence is a cornerstone of our fraternity, we truly believe in aligning our brothers’ career goals with an unparalleled collegiate experience. Traditions are in place to foster a close-knit brotherhood, one made possible by our brothers’ shared aspirations for professional success. We believe that a journey is only as good as the people tagging along, and it is this philosophy that defines our core values.

On a personal note, Alpha Kappa Psi has been instrumental in shaping my professional success, helping me find my sense of purpose in college, and giving me friendships that I cherish - I welcome you to challenge yourself in joining us this fall to see if you can find your place here at USC just as I did.
