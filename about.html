ABOUT THE FRATERNITY

Our History

The Alpha Chapter of Delta Mu Kappa was founded in the Fall of 2017 on Purdue's campus with the goal of forming a strong community of students with an entrepreneurial mindset, regardless of background. Our brothers represent a variety of Purdue schools including Engineering, Science, Management, Polytechnic, Liberal Arts, and more. Through our passion to build the community, we have pioneered events including Startup Weekend Purdue (the first weekend long startup competition on Purdue's campus), Changemakers Purdue (a day long event promoting social entrepreneurship and community development).


We are unique in that we are open to all majors with our brothers and alumni starting their own companies and non profits, going into various industries, and more. We encourage innovation of all kind whether its forming a starting your own venture, community event, internal initiative, and more. Our chapter not only focuses on professional development, but also on service through Habitat builds, educating young students on entrepreneurship, and social events such as a concert in partnership with the College of Liberal Arts.

Our Mission

To foster a perpetual brotherhood of entrepreneurs and innovators dedicated to empowering one another and advancing society.
